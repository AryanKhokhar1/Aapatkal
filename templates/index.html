Welcome to Aapatkal.in!

Aapatkal.in is one the leading application in the Health Care Industry to provide you with all the urgent details regarding medical emergencies in Rajasthan. The aim of this app is to unite the details of Healthcare in a Single-Tap. It provides you all the last minute information regarding any ill situation. Search Bar helps you navigate the best results around your location with all the relevant updates about the availability of blood types, beds, oxygen meters and ambulance. In recent times there are instances where it is difficult to find a hospital with all the necessary requirements for example there are specialized care units for sensitive illness but the patients are not able to find the correct medical help, the at this point this app gives the user a clear view and creates a pre-map to schedule your visit to the hospital with all the authenticated sources and correct details.
